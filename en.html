How do I register on OKX as a beginner?
Visit OKX.com directly and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. You can register with either an email address or phone number. Set a strong password (at least 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, and symbols), verify the code sent to you, then complete KYC (Know Your Customer) identity verification to unlock higher withdrawal limits, P2P trading, and fiat deposits. The full registration takes about 10–15 minutes.
Which crypto exchange has the lowest fees?
Among the top exchanges: OKX offers spot fees starting at 0.08% maker / 0.1% taker, generally the lowest. Binance is 0.1% standard (discounts available with BNB holdings or VIP tier). Bybit is 0.1% for spot, with competitive 0.02%/0.06% maker/taker rates for derivatives. Always check the current fee schedule on the exchange's official website before trading.
Is cryptocurrency trading legal in my country?
Cryptocurrency regulations vary significantly by country. Some countries (like the US, UK, EU, Japan, and Singapore) have clear regulatory frameworks, while others have restrictions or outright bans. Please consult your local laws, tax obligations, and regulations before trading. OuYi Hub is an information site only and does not provide legal or investment advice.
